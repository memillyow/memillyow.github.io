About Me

about_me.exe

Hey! My name is Jamil Omer, but everyone calls me milly. I’m a software engineer with a B.S. in Computer Science (Software Engineering concentration) from Tennessee Technological University.

I first got into coding back when hackers were modding my MW2 lobbies. I’d sit there wondering how they pulled it off—and that curiosity just spiraled into a deep dive that brought me to where I am now. The dream? To make my own game from the ground up. There’s nothing like cracking open an energy drink, firing up Spotify, diving into some code, hitting a wall, losing my mind a bit—and loving every second of it.

You can find just about anything on my website, like projects I'm working on or even what song I'm listening to 0_o. You can also view the important stuff like my GitHub or LinkedIn! Don't get lost!
